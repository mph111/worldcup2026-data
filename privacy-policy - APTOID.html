Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how World Cup 2026 Results (“the App”) handles user information. The App is designed to provide a tournament tracking experience while collecting minimal data.

1. Information Collection

Personal Data: The App does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII), such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise location data.

Local Data Storage: Any match results or preferences you set are stored locally on your device. This data is not uploaded to our servers and is deleted if you uninstall the App.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App is supported by ads to remain free. We use Google AdMob to display banners and interstitial ads. AdMob may collect technical data such as:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID).
  • General usage statistics.

This data is managed by Google. You can review their policy here: Google Privacy Policy.

3. No In-App Purchases

This version of the App, distributed via Aptoide, does not include any in-app purchases or billing systems. All features are available for free through ad support.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is safe for all audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children. Since no personal accounts are required, your privacy is protected by default.

5. Data Security

We use HTTPS to fetch tournament schedules from our server to ensure data integrity. No user-specific data is transmitted during this process.
