Privacy Policy

Nations Cup 2026 Dashboard

Last updated: 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Nations Cup 2026 Dashboard (“the App”) handles user information. Your privacy is important to us. The App is designed to provide a tournament tracking experience while collecting minimal data.

2. Information We Collect

a) Personal Data

The App does not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Photos or files

No personal information is stored or transmitted.

b) Local Data Storage

All tournament data you edit (matches, results, statistics) is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Not uploaded to any server
  • Fully under your control

You may clear this data anytime by uninstalling the App.

c) Internet Access

The App uses internet connection only to:

  • Load online tournament data updates
  • Display advertisements (Google AdMob)

No personal data is transmitted during this process.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • Usage statistics

This data is handled directly by Google according to:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not access or store this advertising data.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time purchase: Remove Ads.

  • All ads are permanently disabled after purchase
  • Payment is processed securely by Google Play Billing
  • We do not receive payment details or financial information

More information:
https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is safe for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child provided personal data, please contact us.

6. Data Security

  • Data is stored locally only
  • No user accounts are required
  • No personal data is transmitted to servers

The risk of data exposure is extremely limited.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Play Billing (payments)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

  • Use the App without providing personal data
  • Delete all stored data by uninstalling the App
  • Disable ads by purchasing Remove Ads

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when needed. Any changes will appear on this page with a new update date.

Email: admin@storeofapps.com
